Good to know

Accommodation

There are a number of places to stay nearby:

The Quay Hotel — premium option, walking distance.

Cambridge Travelodge — affordable option with many other guests staying.

Lodeside Glamping — the nearest affordable option, walkable from the venue.

There are also plenty of Airbnbs around Lode, Reach, and Swaffham Bulbeck.

Travel

The venue is accessible by taxi from both Cambridge station and Cambridge North station.

Parking

Free on-site parking at the barn. Cars can be left overnight.
